Personal Transformation through Occultism

This course is a journey into the hidden realms of the self—where true transformation begins. Together, we will explore the depths of your inner world, uncovering forgotten wisdom and healing what has been left in the dark. This path is for those ready to embrace their full potential, reclaiming lost power and wisdom to step forward with clarity and strength. You will not walk alone. I am here to guide you every step of the way, from the roots of your being to the light of your future.

Blending sidereal astrology, kabbalah, numerology and other methods of divination such as diving into tarot cards and their significance for you personally, how certain placements in your birth chart relate to certain cards and so on..

I crafted a quick 1 month course on how to deeply connect with your cosmic nature through divination. (Note: I do not work with new age systems such as finding which star-seed you embody) instead, I focus on traditional ones.

The course content is as follows:

  • Week 1 - Your tarot card, what it means and where it sits on the tree of life.
  • Week 2 - Your astrological placements in depth, from a sidereal perspective (true to the stars) and how they relate to tarot.
  • Week 3 - Your past life (if you know your exact time of birth)
  • Week 4 - Your purpose (again knowing the time of birth is crucial because of how the nodes (North Node, South Node) are placed through the wheel of astrological houses)
